# Testing Strategy

## Coverage goals

- narrative signal extraction
- clustering and identity shift extraction
- chapter generation
- evolution tracking
- monthly, quarterly, and annual reporting
- HTTP smoke coverage

## Test layers

1. Unit tests for signal extraction and trend logic.
2. Engine tests for narrative ingest and ranking.
3. HTTP smoke tests for the service routes.

## What to verify

- repeated evidence creates a stable narrative
- supporting memories, patterns, emotions, and goals are retained
- belief and value changes are captured in the identity model
- chapters advance as the narrative matures
- annual reporting explains who the person is becoming
